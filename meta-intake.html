2 Where you are in Meta's process

Your answer determines which service path we deliver. We also evaluate your evidence uploads (Section 5) to verify and route you correctly.

Have you already submitted Meta's official appeal?*
"Appeal" includes (a) the in-app Appeal / Disagree / Request Review button on the notice, (b) submitting a Meta response form, or (c) emailing Meta support about this specific issue.
Not sure what counts as an appeal?
Any in-app or emailed response to the notice — "Appeal," "Request a review," "Disagree with decision," "Submit additional information" — counts as your one shot. If you sent ANY response to Meta about this issue, choose "Yes." If you only screenshotted the notice and did nothing, choose "No."
Have you used Meta's internal complaints mechanism (DSA Article 17)?*
Typically the in-app "Appeal" or "Disagree" button on the notice. Required before Appeals Centre Europe is available.
Have you submitted to Appeals Centre Europe (ACE) yet?*
ACE is the DSA Article 21 out-of-court dispute body. Across its first ~1,500 disputes, ACE has reversed Meta in roughly 75% of decisions. Available only after the internal mechanism above.
