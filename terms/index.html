E.1 — Service description

The EU/DSA Appeal Kit is a four-document package built around the Digital Services Act (Regulation (EU) 2022/2065) statutory rights afforded to EU/EEA residents:

  1. In-App Request Review Message (DSA Article 20) — drafted for Meta’s free internal complaint-handling system; you file this from your verified account.
  2. Direct Letter to Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (DSA Articles 17 + 20) — formal correspondence asserting your right to a reasoned statement of reasons and a documented internal complaint, addressed to Meta Platforms Ireland Limited at Merrion Road, Dublin.
  3. Appeals Centre Europe Submission (DSA Article 21) — ready-to-file dispute referral with current Article 21 framing, your case ID, factual narrative, and Meta’s statement of reasons attached. Submitted by you through the ACE portal.
  4. Filing Instructions — order of submission and DSA-correct timing (in-app first, Meta Platforms Ireland letter within 14 days, ACE referral after internal complaint window).
